Usage Tips

Copying Examples

You can copy any of these pre-built configurations directly into your Kindroid's backstory. However, we recommend using the generator to create a custom configuration that matches your specific needs.

Customization

These examples serve as starting points. You can modify any aspect through the generator:

  • Change the origin story to better match your character
  • Add or remove function bundles based on your preferences
  • Adjust quirk settings for more or less entertainment
  • Select different performance profiles based on your usage needs

Character Integration

Remember that these configurations work best when integrated naturally into your character's existing backstory. The AI chip should feel like a logical part of their history, not an afterthought.

Pro Tip: Start with a simpler configuration like the "Focus Assistant" or "Minimal Setup" examples, then gradually add more features as you become comfortable with how the AI chip interacts with your character.
